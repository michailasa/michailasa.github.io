The Restaurant

Canal Bank Cafe has established itself as one of Dublin's favourite all day restaurants. Serving breakfast, lunch and dinner seven days, we have the answer to every dining requirement.

The day starts with our fabulous breakfasts and the eclectic comprehensive main menu kicks in at noon. House favourites include big salads, omelettes, burgers & a variety of steaks and fish dishes. Our daily specials are always worth checking out.

Great cocktails and a large selection of wine and beers ensure that spirits are kept high.

Menu

All meats, poulty & fish are sourced from Irish suppliers, are of Irish origin and are fully traceable from farm to fork. Also where possible all other ingredients are of Irish origin.
